One punch man

June 25, 2019
One Punch Man: an action-comedy manga by ONE and Yusuke Murata. Follows Saitama, an unbeatable hero who defeats enemies with a single punch. Satirical take on superheroes with stunning art and humorous storytelling. Gained popularity for its unique twist on the genre.
