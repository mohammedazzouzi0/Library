Everything is f*cked

2019
"Everything Is Fucked: A Book About Hope" by Mark Manson is a provocative and empowering read that challenges conventional wisdom, explores the paradoxes of life, and encourages readers to find meaning and hope in a chaotic world. It delves into topics like happiness, failure, and mortality, urging introspection and personal responsibility.
