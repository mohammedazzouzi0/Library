Monster

December 1994 and December 2001
Monster manga in the 20th century captivated with chilling narratives and eerie creatures. Junji Ito's creations and Kentaro Miura's "Berserk" showcased horror, suspense, and psychological themes, transporting readers to haunting worlds. This era solidified monster manga as a distinct and thrilling genre.
